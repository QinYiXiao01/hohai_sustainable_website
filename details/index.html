2021城市公园挑战

mid-pacific
1st/2nd

In the post-epidemic era, the demand for socially-distanced activities continue to increase. It has become an urgent task to incorporate epidemic prevention measures in campus landscape design. The design of the site in ASCE university needs to meet this requirement in a more sustainable way.

This paper begins with the team’s sustainable concepts and then identifies the philosophy for the whole sustainable process in order to incorporate it into the site design to obtain sustainability recommendations. Next, combining the site analysis and field research, the existing trees are all reserved and the requirements of both crowd and the site itself are learned as a reference for the general zoning.
