分析

分析，是一切的开始。对场地现状的地形、周边、植被多方面考量， 出于可持续性考虑，尽可能保留原有的植被，因地而制宜。

改造

改造，无论是场地或是其他因素。由设计对其进行改造，并将改造的部分用于新的创造， 从而避免了由于设计本身造成的资源浪费。

设计

设计，涵盖功能性、参与性、可持续性。每一项具体的设计都融入团队的理念并满足Envision Checklist，其形式及内涵上都是独特的。

完善

完善，多方面的修正。设计将从河海本身出发，由潜在的使用者 参与设计，在宣传上也下功夫去做，使其逐步接近完美。

碳排放分析

运用碳排放计算软件SIMAPRO进行的全方位分析计算

With the development of human society, environmental pollution has increasingly become a severe problem and aroused broad concern worldwide. In case of the shortage of resources and energy and the worsening conditions of the environment, life-cycle assessment (LCA) has been proposed for pollution prevention and green design efforts. According to the International Organization for Standardization (ISO) 14040 & 14044, life-cycle assessment can be defined as the compiling and evaluation of the inputs and outputs and the potential environmental impacts of a product system during a product’s lifetime, which gradually becomes a management tool for quantitative analysis and qualitative assessment. Thus, our group's structural carbon analysis is mainly based on the research method of full life-cycle assessment.

径流分析

运用SWMM模型进行场地径流分析计算

The team used the stormwater management model EPA SWMM 5.1 to perform a runoff volume simulation for the site area. The system runoff from the entire site area consisting of pipes, drains, and storage facilities can be calculated using site area data.
The EPA SWMM simulates the surface runoff by dividing it into sub-catchments transported through the established pipe conveyance network to a storage facility and finally to an outfall. The team generalized the site area's drainage system into 32 sub-catchments, 40 stormwater pipe sections, 39 stormwaters well nodes, 2 water storage facilities, and 2 end-of-pipe outfalls.
The team used the Horton infiltration model in the EPA SWMM to simulate stormwater infiltration into the sub-catchments unsaturated upper soil zone. Since the site area consists of USDA Group A soils, we selected a maximum infiltration rate of 12 mm/hr and a minimum infiltration rate of 0.06 mm/hr. for the Horton infiltration model parameters according to the U.S. Soil Classification System

交通分析

运用模拟软件Anylogic进行分析计算

Pedestrian simulation is a common method to analyze the small site roads. Many corresponding theories have been produced in its development, among which "Social Force Model Theory" is the one with good simulation effect. Under the framework of this theory, pedestrians complete the movement from the starting point to the endpoint with the target driving force, the force with obstacles, and other pedestrian forces. Additionally, the acceleration, deceleration, and the social action in walking are considered, making the theory one of the most commonly used pedestrian simulation methods. In addition, in a relatively small field, the behavior of low-speed riders can also be simulated according to pedestrian simulation theory.
Our team used Social Force Model simulation software AnyLogic8.5.0 to analyze the pedestrian flow on the site.
